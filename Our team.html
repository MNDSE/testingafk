Leadership team

Founder: Ndende MBAYE (right) and Co-founder: Vatinel KOUA (left)
have met for the first time through Yali (Young African leadership initiative) during a training session in business and entrepreneurship. After completing the training, they shared the idea of creating a digital media that covers all sort of topics around the continent and allow the young Africans and the voiceless to be heard.
They manage and administrate African King media. They plan the episodes, make decisions and manage the social media pages.
