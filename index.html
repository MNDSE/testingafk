Our mission

AFRICAN KING Podcast is a show whose mission is to inform and awaken the African people, especially its youth.
It will serve as a channel for the voiceless and promote the potential of African youth in all areas.

Our current society is that of information, so whoever has the right information at the right time can move, know, distinguish himself and add value in his field. It is with this in mind that we have created AFRICAN KING to bring our contribution by informing Africa and the rest of the world about our potentialities and the real struggles of the populations.
In addition, we want to serve as a sounding board for the African people, especially those who are muzzled or do not have the opportunity to express themselves on topics that can generate change. We desire to become the number one channel to promote the African youth's talents, realizations, and the favorite channel when it comes to expressing their freedom of expression.
